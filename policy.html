Consent and App Tracking Transparency

Analytics and crash reporting data collection begins only after you explicitly accept our in-app consent screen. Free users may be shown ads; depending on jurisdiction, platform, and user consent choices, those ads may be non-personalized or personalized. The Application uses platform consent tools where applicable.

On iOS 14.5 and later, the Application will present Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt. Your advertising identifier (IDFA) is only shared with AdMob if you grant permission through this prompt. If you deny ATT permission, AdMob serves contextual (non-personalized) ads only.

In regions where additional ad consent is required (such as the EU/EEA under GDPR), the Application may also present Google’s consent form for ad personalization. This form may appear on both iOS and Android. Your response determines whether AdMob may serve personalized ads in those regions.

If you continue without accepting, the Application remains fully functional — gameplay works normally, but analytics and crash reporting are disabled. Advertising may still be shown to free users where permitted by applicable law and platform consent settings.

Legitimate Interest

Depending on the jurisdiction, we may rely on legitimate interest (LGPD Art. 10; GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)) or consent as the legal basis for serving ads to free-tier users to sustain the free version of the Application. In jurisdictions where consent is required for any ad-related processing, the Application obtains that consent through platform-provided mechanisms before serving ads. On iOS, your advertising identifier (IDFA) is only used for ad personalization if you grant ATT permission. On Android, ad personalization is controlled by Google’s consent framework and your device ad settings. You can remove ads entirely by purchasing Ploink Premium.

You may withdraw your consent at any time through the Application settings or by contacting us at ploinkapp@gmail.com. Upon withdrawal, we will stop the consent-based processing going forward.

GDPR (EU/EEA users):

Under the General Data Protection Regulation, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate personal data
  • Request erasure of your personal data
  • Restrict the processing of your personal data
  • Data portability
  • Object to processing of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

CCPA/CPRA (California users):

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected and how it is used
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of the sharing of your personal information
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

You can opt out of such sharing via the iOS ATT prompt, device-level ad settings, or by contacting us at ploinkapp@gmail.com. Note: These rights apply to the extent the CCPA/CPRA is applicable to our processing of your data.

Children

The Application is not directed at children. We do not require account creation, and display names are stored locally on your device and transmitted ephemerally during multiplayer sessions (not retained on our servers beyond the room session).

  • Under 13 (COPPA): We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.
  • Under 12 (LGPD): Under Brazilian law, children are persons under 12. Processing of children’s personal data must be carried out in their best interests and, where required, with the specific and distinguishable consent of at least one parent or legal guardian.
  • 12 to 17 (LGPD): Processing of adolescents’ personal data must be carried out in their best interests, in accordance with applicable Brazilian law.
  • GDPR (EU/EEA): Where processing is based on consent for information society services offered directly to a child, parental authorization may be required below the age threshold set by the relevant Member State, which ranges from 13 to 16.

If we discover that data from a minor has been collected without appropriate consent, it will be deleted immediately. If you are a parent or guardian and are aware that your child has provided personal information, please contact us at ploinkapp@gmail.com.
